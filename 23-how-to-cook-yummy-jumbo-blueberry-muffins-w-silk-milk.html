Jumbo Blueberry Muffins/w silk milk instructions

  2. Add the wet ingredients next....Beat on high for 1 min until silky smooth..
  3. Add the Blueberries. And mix them in with the mixer for 30 seconds. Just enough to break the Blueberries up a bit..
  4. Prepare the streusel mix topping by mixing it together with a fork.

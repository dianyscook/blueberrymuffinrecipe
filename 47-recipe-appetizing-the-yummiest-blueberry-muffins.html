Ingredients of The yummiest blueberry muffins!!!!!!!

  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cups of organic, un bleached flour.
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup of pure white cane sugar.
  3. It's 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  4. You need 2 teaspoons of baking powder.
  5. You need 1/3 cup of olive oil.
  6. It's 1 of egg.
  7. Prepare 1/3 cup of milk.
  8. It's 1 cup of fresh blueberries.
  9. Prepare of White sugar (for sprinkling).

The yummiest blueberry muffins!!!!!!! step by step

  1. Mix together flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder. Set aside..
  2. In a 1 cup measuring cup, add olive oil and egg. Add milk to the cup until it reaches the 1 cup mark.
  3. Add oil mixture to the flour mixture. Fold in blueberries..
  4. Bake for 20- 25 minutes at 400 degrees(mine were a little over cooked at 23 minutes).
  5. Sprinkle with sugar and eat! Enjoy!!!!.
